Additional Services
  • Ohana Special Plus: includes the Ohana Special package along with Full Exterior and Interior services.
  • Full Exterior: includes a car wash, clay treatment, paint enhancement, ceramic sealant, tire dressing, and cleaning of rims, fender wells, and moldings, along with level 1 water spot removal on windows and paint.
  • Full Interior: covers interior vacuuming, mat cleaning, spot cleaning of upholstery, panel dressing, vent and console cleaning, interior window cleaning, and air freshening.
  • Bonus Service: include fabric sealant, leather conditioner, and engine bay detailing.
  • Ceramic Coating Packages: comes with a wash, decontamination, paint correction, and sealant topper, with a 1-2 day turnaround time.
